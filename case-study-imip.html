Editorial Design · Book Layout

IMIP Ciudad Juárez

Interior layout and editorial graphics for an official publication by the Municipal Institute of Planning.

Overview

The Municipal Institute of Planning (IMIP) of Ciudad Juárez published a comprehensive book on urban development and territorial planning. I was commissioned to design the interior layout and create all editorial graphics.

The challenge was to present complex data and policy analysis in a clean, readable format that felt both authoritative and accessible. My work is credited in the published book.

Industry Government / Urban Planning
Services Book Layout, Editorial Graphics, Data Visualization
Timeline 4 Weeks
Deliverables Full Interior Layout, Charts & Graphs, Print-Ready File
Location Ciudad Juárez, México

Design Approach

Purpose

To present urban planning data with clarity and credibility. The design had to serve the content, not compete with it.

Audience

City officials, urban planners, architects, academics, and informed citizens interested in Juárez's development.

Direction

Clean, structured, editorial. Generous margins, clear hierarchy, data presented with precision and restraint.

Core Challenge

Making dense policy language and statistical data visually digestible without losing depth or authority.
